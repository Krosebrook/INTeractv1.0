# INTeract Quick Start Guide
## Welcome to Your Employee Engagement Platform!

---

## Getting Started

### 1. Access the Platform
Open your web browser and go to: **[Your INTeract URL]**

### 2. Log In
1. Click **"Sign In"** on the homepage
2. Enter your work email address
3. Check your email for a magic link
4. Click the link to sign in automatically

> **Tip:** No password needed! We use secure magic links sent to your email.

---

## Your Dashboard

After signing in, you'll see your personal dashboard with:

- **Your Points** - Track your engagement points
- **Recent Activity** - See what's happening in your team
- **Quick Actions** - Common tasks at your fingertips
- **Upcoming Events** - Activities you can join

---

## Main Features

### Give Recognition
Show appreciation to your colleagues!

1. Click **"Give Shoutout"** button
2. Search for your colleague's name
3. Select a category (Teamwork, Innovation, Leadership, etc.)
4. Write a heartfelt message (20+ characters)
5. Optionally add bonus points from your daily allowance
6. Click **"Send Recognition"**

**Categories:**
- Teamwork
- Innovation
- Leadership
- Customer Focus
- Excellence
- Mentorship
- Above & Beyond
- Problem Solving

### View Leaderboards

See who's making an impact!

1. Click **"Leaderboards"** in the navigation
2. Filter by: **Daily | Weekly | Monthly | All Time**
3. View by: **Points | Badges | Participation**
4. See where you rank among colleagues

### Points & Rewards Store

Redeem your earned points!

1. Click **"Rewards"** or **"Point Store"**
2. Browse available rewards
3. Click on a reward to see details
4. Click **"Redeem"** if you have enough points
5. Follow instructions to claim your reward

**Point Values:**
- Giving recognition: +5 points
- Receiving recognition: +10-35 points
- Completing activities: Varies
- Earning badges: Bonus points

### Events & Activities

Join team activities!

1. Click **"Events"** or **"Calendar"**
2. Browse upcoming activities
3. Click an event to see details
4. Click **"Join"** or **"RSVP"**
5. Add to your calendar
